About Us

Dedicated to bringing peace of mind to South African families since 2014

Our Story

Founded by a mother who understood the struggle of finding trustworthy childcare.

Enary Helpers & Nannies was born from a personal need. Our founder, herself a working mother, experienced firsthand the anxiety of leaving children with strangers and the difficulty of finding reliable, vetted caregivers.

What started as a small network of trusted nannies in Pretoria has grown into a comprehensive placement service serving families across South Africa. Today, we've helped over 500 families find the perfect caregivers, and we've provided meaningful employment to hundreds of qualified helpers.

Our name "Enary" comes from the concept of "enary" - meaning a group of nine, representing the nine provinces of South Africa. We're proud to serve families nationwide.

Our Mission

To connect South African families with compassionate, qualified caregivers who provide exceptional care and bring peace of mind to every home we serve.

Our Vision

To be South Africa's most trusted name in childcare and home help, setting the standard for quality, safety, and professionalism in the care industry.
