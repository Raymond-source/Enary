Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you find me a nanny?

Typically within 3-7 business days, depending on your specific requirements. For emergency care, we can often provide same-day service.

What is your vetting process?

All candidates undergo background checks, reference verification, qualification verification, and in-person interviews. We also check ID documents and police clearance where applicable.

What are your service fees?

Our placement fee varies based on the type of service and duration. Contact us for a personalized quote - we offer competitive rates and payment plans.

Do you offer replacement guarantees?

Yes! If you're not completely satisfied with your placement within the first month, we'll provide a replacement at no additional cost.
